Our Apps

Kandl Studio builds simple, privacy-focused utility apps for iOS and iPadOS. All our apps are free to use, contain no ads, and collect no personal data.

General FAQ

Do your apps collect any personal data?

No. All Kandl Studio apps are designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Which devices are supported?

Our apps support iPhone and iPad running iOS/iPadOS 17.0 or later. Please check each app's App Store page for specific device requirements.

The app is not working properly. What should I do?

Please try the following steps:
1. Make sure your device is running the latest iOS version.
2. Force-close the app and reopen it.
3. Restart your device.
4. Reinstall the app from the App Store.
If the issue persists, please contact us via email below.

How do I request a new feature?

We'd love to hear your ideas! Send us an email with a description of the feature you'd like, and we'll consider it for a future update.
