Flip Clock

Support & Help

About the App

Flip Clock transforms your iPad into an elegant desk clock with a classic split-flap animation. Place your iPad horizontally on a stand and enjoy a beautiful ambient display showing the current time.

How to Use

  • Launch the app — the clock starts immediately.
  • Place your iPad in landscape orientation on a desk stand.
  • No setup or configuration is required.
  • The screen stays on automatically while the app is open.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why does the screen stay on even after a while?

Flip Clock disables the auto-lock feature while the app is running so it can serve as a desk clock. Screen auto-lock resumes normally when you leave the app.

Does the app display seconds?

The current version displays hours and minutes only, optimised for a clean, readable desk display. Seconds are intentionally omitted to reduce animation frequency and battery consumption.

Does the app work on iPhone?

Flip Clock is designed exclusively for iPad in landscape orientation. It is not available for iPhone.

Does the app collect any personal data?

No. Flip Clock does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. See our Privacy Policy for details.

The flip animation looks choppy. What can I do?

Make sure your iPad is not in Low Power Mode, as this can reduce animation frame rate. Closing other apps running in the background may also help.
